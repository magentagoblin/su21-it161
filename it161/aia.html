Audience, Issues, and Approach

Client Name (Final Decision Maker) Big Leather (Leo Rauf)
Client Business/Website Name Big Leather
Redesign or brand new website? Brand new website!
Type of Website Indetity/Entertainment (Artist/Band)
Client's Goals To gain a bigger online fanbase for their band, to stream their music online, to showcase their art, to inform their audience of band news, and to sell their albums/art.
Audiences Issues, Concerns, Needs Approach
Returning Fans

Their current fanbase is mostly aged 20-40 years old, low income, fellow artists or local (New Orleans LA) fans, tend to have moderate-high skill with navigating websites, and low-average internet bandwith.
  • Wants to keep track of the band's show shedule and news (i.e. new album release)
  • Needs to be able to purchase albums, art, or donate to Big Leather
  • Wants to message or send fan art to Big Leather
  • Big Leather newsletter page with possible email notifications for users
  • Calender page for Big Leather shows and important dates
  • Shopping page with client's albums and art; Include place to donate to client directly
  • Contact form for users to message client (allow image/audio/video files in messages for fanart)
New Fans

Their main target audience for new fans is really anyone/everyone (mainly 18+ years old as only legal adults will be able to actually purchase albums online), including people with poor website skills and all incomes. They also want these new fans to largely be outside of New Orleans (Big Leather's base city), as this a goal of this website is to extend their reach of audiences.
  • Needs to learn about the band and its members
  • Needs to be able to find any streaming platform where they can listen to Big Leather
  • Needs to be able to purchase albums, art, or donate to Big Leather
  • Wants to message or send fan art to Big Leather
  • Biography/history page about Big Leather. Include photos and a bio for each band member
  • Links on site's homepage to client's music pages on Bandcamp, Spotify, and YouTube
  • Shopping page with client's albums and art; Include place to donate to client directly
  • Contact form for users to message client (allow image/audio/video files in messages for fanart)
Talent Seekers

Music producers, event/gig coordinators, record labels and/or music store owners are all a major target audience. This audience will be mostly 30+ years old, mostly higher education/income, mostly savvy website navigating skills, and will have good internet bandwith.
  • Needs to learn about the band and its members
  • Needs to be able to find any streaming platform where they can listen to Big Leather
  • Needs to easily contact Big Leather about gigs or collaborations
  • Probably wants to see a show schedule and/or any recent news from Big Leather
  • Biography/history page about Big Leather. Include photos and a bio for each band member. Additionally include section addressing talent seekers and client's wish to be hired and/or collaborate
  • Links on site's homepage to client's music pages on Bandcamp, Spotify, and YouTube
  • Contact form for users to message client with ability to include reply contact info, subject line, and sending files to the client
  • Calender page for Big Leather shows, and page for all band news
